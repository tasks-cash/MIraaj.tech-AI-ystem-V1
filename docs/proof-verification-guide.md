# Proof verification

Proof screenshots are private evidence, not absolute proof. The worker validates the assignment and deadline, reads private MinIO evidence, runs local OCR and QR decoding, compares the marker, copy, disclosure and group, checks exact/perceptual reuse, records bounded manipulation indicators, and calculates deterministic scores.

Automatic verification is disabled by default. Private groups, uncertain OCR, cropped evidence, ambiguous groups and manipulation indicators require human review. Wrong-assignment QR, exact cross-user reuse, expiration, missing disclosure and unapproved links reject deterministically. Review decisions are immutable and audited.

No face recognition, identity inference, social credential storage, private scraping, or fabricated evidence is implemented.
